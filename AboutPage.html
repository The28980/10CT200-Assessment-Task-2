• WHAT IS BIG MOTHER

    ‘Big Mother’ is a social media project that hopes to help people encourage each other to live healthier lives. ‘Big Mother’ gets its name from the infamous ‘Big Brother’ from George Orwell’s classic ‘1984.’ But instead of an all-seeing brother that dictates your every action, Big Mother is an all-seeing caretaker that seems petulantly insistent that you live life healthily. Through this app, you may work as an agent of Big Mother to spread propaganda about living healthily. Glory to healthy living!
